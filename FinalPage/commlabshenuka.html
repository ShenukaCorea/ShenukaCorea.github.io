SHENUKA'S COMM LAB !
When SHENUKA isn’t travelling, you can catch her cosplaying and selling art at Comic Cons.

Raised among the ancient temples of Sri Lanka and the creatures of arid scrublands, tropical rainforest and temperate mountains, Shenuka stepped off a plane one January afternoon thinking ‘I’m making a big mistake’.

She is influenced by mythology, art history, her heritage and countless hours she spends holed up in her room with internet.

She is an illustrator and character designer who also writes and illustrates her own comics.
